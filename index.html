About Dibrugarh University Badminton Stadium

The Dibrugarh University badminton stadium is a modern, state-of-the-art sports facility that is open to students, faculty, and staff of the university. It is equipped with top-of-the-line badminton courts and amenities, making it an ideal place for players of all levels to practice and compete. In addition to being a hub for badminton, the stadium is also home to a variety of other sports and fitness programs, including yoga and aerobics classes. The stadium is committed to promoting a healthy, active lifestyle and offers something for everyone.
